UPFRONT COSTS FOR BUYING PROPERTY IN UAE

On average, buying a property in Dubai requires approximately 7% of the property price as upfront cost.

Breakdown:

1. Dubai Land Department (DLD) Transfer Fee:
   - 4% of the property purchase price.
   - Mandatory for all property transfers.

2. DLD Admin Fee:
   - AED 540 on ready property transactions.

3. Trustee Registration Office Fee:
   - AED 4,000 for properties above AED 500,000.
   - AED 2,100 for properties under AED 500,000.

4. Mortgage Registration Fee:
   - 0.25% of the loan amount.
   - Additional AED 290 as admin charge.

5. Bank Processing Fee:
   - Around 1% of mortgage loan amount.
   - Some banks may charge AED 2,000–3,000 processing charge.

6. Valuation Fee:
   - AED 2,500–3,500 depending on the bank.

7. Broker Commission:
   - Optional (usually 2% of property price) if using an agent.

AVERAGE ESTIMATE:
Total Upfront Costs ≈ 5% – 8% of property price.
Use 7% in calculations to keep estimates consistent.

NOTES:
- Costs vary by emirate and property type.
- Off-plan projects may require different schedules.
